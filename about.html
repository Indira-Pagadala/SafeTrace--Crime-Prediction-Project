Vision

Empowering safer communities through data-driven insights and predictions in-turn fostering a future where proactive preventive measures combat crime and spread awareness leading to global safety in the long term.

Mission

At SafeTrace, our mission is to harness the power of advanced machine learning and geospatial intelligence to predict and analyze crime rates in India annually. We aim to provide comprehensive insights into crime trends, enabling informed decision-making and collaborative efforts towards fostering safer environments

What we do?

SafeTrace leverages cutting-edge technologies like machine learning and geospatial intelligence & focuses on predicting and analyzing annual crime rates in India. Our sophisticated algorithms enable us to discern patterns and trends, offering valuable insights into the dynamics of crime across various states. By empowering users with informed data analysis, we aspire to create a proactive approach towards addressing societal safety concerns

How We Do?

At SafeTrace, we specialize in predicting and analyzing crime rates across different states in India. By utilizing user-provided year and state data, our advanced machine learning models generate comprehensive reports and visual representations of crime trends. Our platform offers users the ability to explore and understand the changing landscape of crime, enabling them to become informed individuals and contribute towards building safer communities.
